Privacy Policy — LIFELINK

Last updated: June 2026 | Operated by ETHAN Studio

This Privacy Policy explains how ETHAN Studio collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the LIFELINK game.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in using Facebook, Google, or email, we may collect: your name, profile picture, email address, a unique account ID, and in-game data such as your character, progress, and gameplay activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

To create and authenticate your account, save your progress, run and improve the game, and contact you about your account when needed.

3. Data Storage

Your account and game data are stored securely on our game servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We only share data with login providers (Facebook, Google) to authenticate you, our hosting provider to store data, and authorities if required by law.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

LIFELINK is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; changes are posted here with an updated date.

8. Contact

openworldlifelink@gmail.com

User Data Deletion

To delete your LIFELINK account and all personal data:

  1. Email openworldlifelink@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Delete My Account"
  3. Tell us the email or login (Facebook/Google) you used.

We will permanently delete your account and data within 30 days and confirm by email.
