Privacy Policy

LIFELINK is an open-world social game. This policy explains, in plain language, what we collect when you play, why we collect it, and the control you have over your data.

Last updated: June 2026 Applies to: LIFELINK (Android & PC) Operated by ETHAN Studio

This Privacy Policy describes how ETHAN Studio ("we", "us", "our") handles your information when you create an account and play LIFELINK ("the Game"). We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the Game, and we never sell your personal information. By playing LIFELINK, you agree to the practices described below.

1

Information we collect

When you sign in using Facebook, Google, or email, and as you play, we may collect:

  • Account basics — your name and profile picture from your chosen sign-in provider.
  • Email address — used to identify your account and contact you when needed.
  • A unique account ID — so we can recognise you each time you log in.
  • In-game data — your character, progress, settings, and gameplay activity inside the world.
  • Basic technical data — device and connection information needed to run the Game reliably.
2

How we use your information

  • To create your account and sign you in securely.
  • To save your progress and deliver the multiplayer experience.
  • To operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Game.
  • To contact you about your account or important service changes.
  • To keep the world safe and enforce our rules against abuse or cheating.
3

How your data is stored & protected

Your account and game data are stored on secure game servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. We keep your data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to operate the Game.

4

How we share data

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Sign-in providers (Facebook, Google) — strictly to authenticate you.
  • Hosting & infrastructure providers — to store and run your account and game data on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements — when required by applicable law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Other players — your public display name and in-world presence are visible to others, as expected in a social game.
5

Your rights & choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. You may also withdraw a sign-in connection from your provider's settings. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

6

Children's privacy

LIFELINK is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

7

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Game evolves. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued play after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8

Delete your account & data

You can permanently delete your LIFELINK account and the personal data linked to it at any time. There are two ways to do it — choose whichever is easier for you.

Fastest

Option 1 — Inside the game

  1. Open LIFELINK and go to Settings → Account.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are removed right away.
No app needed

Option 2 — By email

  1. Email openworldlifelink@gmail.com.
  2. Subject: "Delete My Account".
  3. Tell us the email or sign-in (Facebook / Google) you used.
✉ Request deletion by email

What gets deleted, and what we keep

  • Deleted — your account, profile (name & picture), email, account ID, character, and all gameplay data.
  • Temporarily kept — limited records we are legally required to retain (for example, fraud prevention or abuse enforcement) are stored separately and removed once the required period ends.

Email requests are completed within 30 days of verifying your identity, and we confirm by email once done. In-app deletion takes effect immediately. We also instruct our hosting providers to delete the corresponding data.
